Red Packet Design

Red packets, also known as "hongbao" or "lai see," are traditional gifts that are given during the Chinese New Year to bring good luck and prosperity.

To design my red packets, I used bright and vibrant colors to convey the joy and excitement of the Chinese New Year. I also incorporated traditional Chinese symbols and patterns, such as red lanterns, gold coins, and the Chinese character for "good fortune."

Overall, I am proud of my red packet designs and I hope that they bring a bit of joy and cheer to people during this challenging time.
